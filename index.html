Our Identity

Three values, written into our name.

Each letter of RAC reflects something we hold dear — about who we are, where we are, and whose we are.

R

Rainier

Located in one of the most ethnically diverse zip codes in the United States, we celebrate the many cultures God has brought together here, and pursue reconciliation and justice as biblical values.

A

Avenue

We've been on this corner since 1904. We take that geographic presence seriously — to know, love, and bless this neighborhood, and to be a blessing to the city around us.

C

Church

Above all, we are part of the Body of Christ — guided by Scripture, rooted in theological orthodoxy, and committed to following Jesus together as a worshipping family.

Our Story

120 years on the same corner — and still becoming.

From 1904 to today, Rainier Avenue Church has been one of the longest-standing institutions of Hillman City. Our congregation is culturally, linguistically, and chronologically diverse — by intention, not by accident.

We believe diversity is a gift from God that reflects His love for all people, and one that requires our full commitment. As a Free Methodist congregation, we draw on a Wesleyan heritage that emphasizes serving the poor, ordaining women, and a longstanding witness for justice and freedom.
