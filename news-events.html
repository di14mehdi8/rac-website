Weekly worship, recurring ministries, and the gatherings that mark the rhythm of life together at Rainier Avenue Church.

Every Week

Our weekly rhythm.

Sundays · 10:00 AM

Family Worship

Our central weekly gathering — multicultural, multigenerational worship grounded in Scripture and the sacraments. All are welcome.

Sundays · 10:00 AM

Children's Worship

A safe, joyful, age-appropriate environment for children from infants through 5th grade, running concurrently with Family Worship.

Wednesdays · 7:00 PM

Youth Worship

A weekly gathering for middle and high schoolers. Also meets 1st & 3rd Sundays at 10 AM during the school year.

Featured

10K Against Breast Cancer

Join the Rainier Avenue Church community as we walk together to raise awareness and support for those affected by breast cancer.

This annual community event is a chance to step outside, move with friends and neighbors, and remember together that the work of healing belongs to us all. Registration details are circulated through Sunday announcements and our weekly email.
